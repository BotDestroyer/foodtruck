About


Founded in 2019, Gabby's Sweet Treats is the brainchild of a father-daughter team, Ollie and Gabby. As a passionate athlete, Gabby is often at sporting events as a participant and spectator. She began to notice that the selection of snacks and refreshments were not the greatest. This was disappointing to Gabby- she believes that having a treat at a track meet or football game makes the experience that much better. Gabby knew that there was a need for it, and Gabby's Sweet Treats was created!


We pride ourselves on enhancing sporting events with the presence of amazing desserts. “The SweetLifeTruck” visits are also the perfect addition for parties, fairs, and PTA sponsored events. On The SweetLifeTruck, we have a wide selection including all time favorites like ice cream cones and italian ice. Or, some choose to indulge in our custom menu offerings like the Cereal Bowl or a decadent slice of cheesecake. There is always something for everyone. We do carry gluten-free, dairy-free and organic options!


Ollie and Gabby are dedicated to bringing the fun and innovation to creative desserts to reach children and families in the DMV area. Let The SweetLifeTruck provide a special visit to your next event!
